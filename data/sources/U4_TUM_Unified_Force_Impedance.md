# Unified force-impedance control

* **ID:** U4
* **Source:** Technical University of Munich (TUM)
* **URL:** https://portal.fis.tum.de/en/publications/unified-force-impedance-control/

## Summary and Conclusions
This publication focuses on unified force-impedance control (UFIC). It discusses integrating the advantages of direct force control and impedance control. This framework is essential for handling contact/non-contact transitions gracefully and maintaining stable contact during ultrasound scanning, especially when the probe undergoes sudden changes in tissue contact.
