# Hybrid Position-Force Control

* **ID:** U3
* **Source:** ScienceDirect Topics
* **URL:** https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/engineering/hybrid-position-force-control

## Summary and Conclusions
This reference page outlines hybrid position-force control. It explains the decomposition of task space into two orthogonal subspaces: the force-controlled subspace (typically normal to the contact surface, where constant contact force must be maintained) and the position/velocity-controlled subspace (tangential to the surface, along which the robot scans/moves). It forms the mathematical foundation of automated scanning systems.
